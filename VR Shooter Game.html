    VR Shooter Game

    VR Shooter Game is the zombie shooter game, it will be the shooting game when player can take a gun and melee on the ground then they can run to shoot the enemy on the street until get to the end of the map then it will be win. The priority of this game is entertaining player can shoot a lot of bullet into the zombie body and feel the timing when the bullet hit the zombie.
