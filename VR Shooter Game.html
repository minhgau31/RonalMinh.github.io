    Why VR Game

VR is the platform that I heard it for a long time ago, but I don't have VR Headset, and I haven't tried it yet, so this time will be the chance for me to approach VR. VR will be an exciting gaming platform in the future because a big company is starting to do something in VR, so I want to join it before it is too late.

    VR Shooter Game

    VR Shooter Game is a zombie shooter game. It will be a shooting game where players can take a gun and melee on the ground and then run to shoot the enemy on the street until they get to the end of the map. Then it will win. The priority of this game is entertainment, for a player can shoot a lot of bullets into the zombie body and feel the timing when the bullet hits the zombie.
